About Salchester Arms

Welcome to Salchester Arms, where luxury meets tranquility in the heart of the English countryside. Our hotel offers 50 well-appointed bedrooms, a stunning restaurant and bar, and state-of-the-art conference facilities. Whether you're here for a relaxing getaway or a corporate event, we strive to provide a memorable experience.

Our History

Salchester Arms has been a family-owned establishment since its inception. Founded by Sally Chester and her family, our hotel has a rich history of providing exceptional hospitality to guests from near and far. Over the years, we have grown to become a beloved destination for those seeking a peaceful retreat.

Our Mission

At Salchester Arms, our mission is to create a welcoming and comfortable environment for our guests. We are committed to sustainability, sourcing local produce, and providing top-notch service. Whether you're here for a leisurely stay or hosting a corporate event, we aim to exceed your expectations.
